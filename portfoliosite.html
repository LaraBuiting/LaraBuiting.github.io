Portfolio website

For a school project I did my first try at making a portfolio website. Since I was still in my first year, I had not had too much experience with HTML and CSS but in the end, it did turn out to be nice. I learned a lot from it. I wanted to have my games playable on this site. So, I made WebGL versions of my games and embedded them into my website

It is about showing my projects and making a place where someone can easily find my projects. I learned how to embed games into my website and make a contact option.

Programs I used:

I used HTML and CSS for this with Visual Studio. I also used Adobe XD for my wireframe and Pinterest together with adobeXD for the mood board.

Progress images

First I made my mood board for this website, I wanted it to be colorful and used these colors because I like pastel colors. After that I made the wireframe, then started to make my about me page, finished it. Then added the games and made pages for them. And made a contact page.
