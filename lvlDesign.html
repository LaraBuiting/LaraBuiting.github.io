For our chosen class we had to make a level design of a game we liked or just any kind of game. To figure out what makes a good level design. I decided I wanted to make a level for an escape room game. So with unity I made a witch hut type of house. And also hid some keys and chests for people to solve and escape the house.
Down here you can see the layout of my level.

You start in the bedroom of the cabin. In the first image you can see the bedroom as normal and in the second you can see the chest open with a key inside. The keys are colorcoded so the chest has blue on it. Which means that only the blue key fits, same as for the door its marked with red so only the red key.

The second room you get to is the hallway. There is a lot of storage and you may have to move a few things to solve it.

The third room is the livingroom. There you see a lot of books and a few other decorations.
