For our chosen class we had to make a level design of a game we liked or just any kind of game. To figure out what makes a good level design. I decided I wanted to make a level for an escape room game. So with unity I made a witch hut type of house. And also hid some keys and chests for people to solve and escape the house.
Down here you can see the layout of my level.

It is about an adventurer exploring a Mystical world with slimes who are trying to attack the village and the whole world. I made the Enemy AI controls, Camera Movement, Health bar, Map creation, Main menu, Logo, and the Game over screen. I learned how to make the enemies move in a certain pattern and attack the player when they come near.
