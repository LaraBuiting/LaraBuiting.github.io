Who am I?

My name is Lara Buiting, I am a 17-year-old software development student from the Netherlands. When I am not working with code or busy programming, I like to make illustrations or play games with my friends. I have experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C#, and I am still eager to learn more.

How did I get interested in programming?

When I was younger, I grew up with a dad who was already in the coding industry and a twin brother who was interested in learning to code a bit and figuring out how the games we liked playing worked or were made. So, we tried making our own games with Scratch which was not really code but a block-based visual language. But it was sparking something in me, maybe I want to do something with this. So, when I had to choose an extra subject in high school, I thought, “Well, I am interested in coding but do not know much about it maybe this is my time to see if this is something for me”. That was the first time I got in contact with HTML and CSS. I really liked it; I liked designing and making the blank page look cool with just my code.

What am I doing now?

When I got to my current school, I learned a lot of different things and am still learning, I learned to make some simple 2D platformers, make better websites. But what I think is the coolest is learning and figuring out how to solve problems. Even when they are harder, I really get joy out of struggling and then figuring something out or making the inner workings of a game or app. So, for what kind of developer I would want to be I think I would want to become a backend developer.
