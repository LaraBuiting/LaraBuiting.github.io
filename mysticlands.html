My friend and I were trying to make a mobile game, this was our first time making a joystick and a full map. We made a top-down mobile game which is tested on our phones inspired by Zelda: Link to the past and Stardew Valley. You can fight slimes and explore the world.

Unity

VisualStudio Code

C#

It is about an adventurer exploring a Mystical world with slimes who are trying to attack the village and the whole world. I made the Enemy AI controls, Camera Movement, Health bar, Map creation, Main menu, Logo, and the Game over screen. I learned how to make the enemies move in a certain pattern and attack the player when they come near.
