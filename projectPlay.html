In class we were going to make a game together with some of the designers and another software developer. We called it Go-Go Gulpz which was about selling energy drinks.

The designers made all the sprites and I implemented all the colliders, player walking, animations, dialogue code, NPC interactions, pickup mechanics, energy depleting code and background music.
Down here you can play the game use WASD to walk E to drink energy and F to talk to people (click after a sentence has finished to go to the next)
!!----!! Please when playing do not press F multiple times to talk to NPC's it will mess up the dialogue !!----!!
